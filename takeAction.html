How Can We Help the Environment?

You may be wondering "is there anything I can do to help conserve the environment". Well you can! Some of things you can do to protect our environment and the wildlife surrounding it are:

  • Conserve Water

    • This means to use less water if possible, or in other words, don't waste water, which would protect the amount of water in our oceans.

  • Reduce Reuse Recycle (triple r)

    • We may have heard this term in many places, such as schools or in our communities. This means to simply recycle objects and sort them out appropriately, rather than literring.

  • Walk,bike,or use MTA

    • By walking or biking, we are lessening the amount of fuels and gases that are escaping into the air through driving.

  • Save Energy at Home

    • To save energy at home, try to turn off the lights when they are not needed!

  • Volunteer

    • Volunteering in your local park can go a long way! Being a volunteer to help clean up and pick up trash can help to ensure that the environment is getting cleaned and even inspire others to join the cause!
